[ Cyperpunk Headwear ]

The future of augmented reality seems to be eyeglasses. It will change the future of the metaverse, and shape the world we live in its current state. Though, it's still hard for me to imagine that shortly humans will get used to wearing the device.





After humans overcome the metaverse hurdle, what will the next future world be? "Cyberpunk" may be the answer. Humans and machines live together through intelligent sensors; Smartphones and screens, become fashionable wearable accessories.

For the 3d scanning printing assignment this week, I try redesigning the VR headset to align with my imagination of the cyberpunk world. At first, I used the Polycam App to scan an oculus to get a sense of the object scale to remodel it more easily.





The next stage is redesigning the appearance of the device. I do not want to block people's sight like a traditional VR headset, so I adjusted the wearing angle to be worn on the forehead like goggles. The smartphone can easily "slide" into this wearable device to be the second screen of your body.
