Stepless is a modular-discrete stair, based on a computational understanding of the interlocking toy brick. Inspired by M.C. Escher "Relativity", Stairs, an indispensable element for connecting spaces, become surreal and symmetrical. I reinterpret the meaning of stairs, as it will gradually lose its functionality, which are the "missing steps" in this case.

Stepless is assembled with easily understandable digital logic without additional adhesive. The pieces are modeled in rhino using grasshopper to parameterize the width, length, including considering material tolerance. It is a scalable, open-ended structure and versatile as digital data.
